Who We Are

Our Team

We are Charlottesville Alliance FC. Our mission is to be a community asset by creating a nationally recognized soccer team. Community, Diversity and Fun are our core values.

Our Home Field

Our matches will be played on Deberry-Bingler Field at Charlottesville High School located in the heart of the city and boasts a beautiful field that can hold 2000 or more fans.

Our League

We represent Charlottesville in the NPSL league's Northeast region: Mid-Atlantic Conference. NPSL is the largest adult league in the United States.
